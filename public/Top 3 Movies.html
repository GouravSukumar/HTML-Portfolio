The Best Movies According To Gourav

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction drama film directed by Sir Christopher Nolan,
who co-wrote the screenplay with his brother Jonathan.

Inception

Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film written and directed by Christopher Nolan,
who also produced it with Emma Thomas, his wife.
The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a professional thief
who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets

Tenet
